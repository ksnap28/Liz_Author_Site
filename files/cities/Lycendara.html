Country

Lycendara is the capital of the Dominion of Lycenda.

People

People from this city are called Lycendaran and people from this country are called Lycendan.

Form of Government

Lycenda is a military dictatorship. It is led by a general called the olantha.

Current Leader

Olantha Erabos Catiando

Location

Lycendara was built where the Tegas River flows into the Lyce river. The city is in a river valley, but it is surrounded by mountains.

Notable Features

Lycendara has more than a dozen towers rise above the city. The Citadel, the center of the Lycendan government, sits on an island where the Lyce and Tegas rivers meet.

Population

Lycendan citizens make up a minority of the population. Most residents are Malisans (slaves) or Perokoi (non-citizen merchants and craftsmen). Immigrants are not welcome.
