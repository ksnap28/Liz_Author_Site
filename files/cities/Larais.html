Country

Larais is the capital of the Republic of Ythome.

People

People from this city are called Laraian and people from this country are called Ythomin.

Form of Government

Ythome is a Republic. It is led by the Syndera Council. The head of the council is called the kyriotha.

Current Leader

Kyriotha Tisander Alamedon

Location

Larais sits at the end of a narrow peninsula. It was built on the slopes of Mt. Gnosi. Caves, both natural and manmade, burrow deep into the mountain. Much of the city is underground.

Notable Features

Most houses in Larais are whitewashed with bright blue roofs. The Vivliotas, a massive library carved directly into the mountain, sits near the peak of Mt. Gnosi.

Population

About 200,000 people live in Larais. It is a cosmopolitan city which welcomes immigrants from all over Gaia.

Notable Festivals

Paiza- a spring festival of renewal. A man and woman are chosen to represent the god Thao and his wife, Sophia. The city then spends five days worshipping them with wine and wildflowers.
