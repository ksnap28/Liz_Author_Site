Country

Agier is the capital of the Empire of Thao.

People

People from this city are called Agieran and people from this country are called Thavean.

Form of Government

The Empire of Thao is a theocracy. It is led by the thaveotha (the leader of the Thavean religion).

Current Leader

Thaveotha Linos Antares

Location

The city of Agier is situated in the foothills of Mt. Sol.

Notable Features

The Temple of the Sun sits at the center of town.

Population

About 50,000 people live in Agier. There are few immigrants. Most people are Thavean citizens.

Culture

The city is overtly religious but many sins are tolerated in private.
