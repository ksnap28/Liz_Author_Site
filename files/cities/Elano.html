Country

Elano is a small town in the northern part of the Republic of Ythome.

People

People from this city are called Elano and people from this country are called Ythomin.

Form of Government

Ythome is a Republic. Elano is led by a kyrios, the elected representative to the Syndera Council in Larais.

Current Leader

Kyrios Pirro

Location

Elano is nestled between the foothills of the Dioni mountains and the Crescent Sea. A reef begins a few miles offshore.

Population

About 5,000 people live in Elano. Most families have lived here for generations

Culture

Most activity is focused on the sea, but the city also boasts several popular story-tellers.
