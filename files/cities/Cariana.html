Country

Cariana is the capital of the Kingdom of Olenos.

People

People from this city are called Carianan and people from this country are called Olenosi.

Form of Government

The Kingdom of Olenos is a Monarchy.

Current Leader

Prince Ryk (regent)

Location

Cariana was built on the shore of the Nevma Gulf. The city is relatively flat.

Notable Features

The Tomb of Ages is a large crypt with extensive underground catacombs. A sprawling slum called Tarantus stretches west from the city walls.

Population

About 150,000 people live in Cariana and its surroundings. Only Olenosi citizens may live in the city. Outside the walls, in the Tarantus slums, Olenosi citizens live alongside Lycendan exiles.

Culture

It is believed this is where the boundary between Gaia and the Other World (the afterlife) is thinnest. Many fortune-tellers and famoiae (mediums) make a living here.

Notable Festivals

Famonychte- the Night of Souls. A celebration of the dead. Hundreds, sometimes thousands gather at the Tomb of Ages bearing gifts of hyacinths or food in the hope they will see a deceased loved one.
