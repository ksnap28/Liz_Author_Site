Country

Maripessa is the capital of the Republic of Grava.

People

People from this city are called Maripessan and people from this country are called Gravan.

Form of Government

Grava is nominally a Republic. It is led by the Voulis Council. Members are not elected. They are chosen by lot.

Current Leader

Keeper of Lots Derius Giatri

Location

Maripessa is on the northern shore of the Strait of Ennekos. The city is hilly but not mountainous.

Notable Features

The Naia Palace, an ancient castle, sits atop a hill overlooking the Strait of Ennekos.

Population

About 200,000 people live in Maripessa. It is a cosmopolitan city which welcomes immigrants from all over Gaia.

Culture

Maripessa prides itself on its art and poetry, but it is most famous for its brothels and pleasure houses.

Notable Festivals

Republica- a celebration of the revolution against the former monarchs. Revelers dress in rags and masks and dance the night away.
